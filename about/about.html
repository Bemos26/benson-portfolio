Meet Benson Mose

Benson Mose is a developer, designer, and musician based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a foundation in mathematics and computer science, he blends technical precision with creative expression to build solutions that are both functional and beautiful.

His journey spans multiple domains — from backend development and data visualization to graphic design and music arrangement. Benson thrives on understanding systems deeply, whether he's debugging C code, customizing his development environment, or conducting a brass ensemble.

He’s known for his resourcefulness and clarity: organizing his workspace with custom folders and PATH management, troubleshooting hardware and software issues with ease, and always asking “why” to get to the root of a problem.

Beyond the screen, Benson is a national-level musician, having performed and led bands at prestigious festivals. His trumpet and piano skills are matched by his ability to arrange music and guide ensembles with precision and passion.

He integrates philosophical and spiritual frameworks into his work, drawing from biblical principles and daily practices like prayer to stay grounded and intentional. His approach to learning is holistic — technical, creative, and spiritual.

Recent additions to his toolkit include Dart, Flutter, and Canva, which he uses to build cross-platform apps and design visuals for educational platforms and personal branding. He’s also exploring prompt engineering and AI collaboration to enhance productivity and creativity.

Whether building a portfolio, mentoring peers, or reflecting on the future of programming, Benson is driven by curiosity and built for impact.

“Driven by curiosity. Built for impact.”
